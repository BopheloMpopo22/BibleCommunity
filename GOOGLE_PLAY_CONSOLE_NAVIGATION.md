### Method 1: Using the Left Sidebar (Most Common)

   - App icon upload
   - Feature graphic upload
   - Screenshots section
   - Description fields
   - Privacy Policy URL
   - Support email

### Method 2: If Sidebar is Hidden

1. **Look for the menu icon** (â˜°) in the **top-left corner**

3. **Click "Store presence"** or **"Store listing"**

### Method 3: Using the URL

1. **Check your browser's address bar**
2. **It should say something like:** `https://play.google.com/console/u/0/developers/.../app/.../store-listing`
3. **If it doesn't say "store-listing"**, manually navigate:
   - Look for your app name in the top navigation
   - Click on it
   - Then click "Store presence" from the left sidebar
