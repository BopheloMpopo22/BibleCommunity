Child Safety Standards

Last Updated: January 2025

At Bible Community, we are committed to protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform. This document outlines our safety standards, reporting procedures, and commitment to child safety.

1. Our Commitment to Child Safety

Bible Community is committed to:

  • Preventing child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and exploitation on our platform
  • Providing clear reporting mechanisms for child safety concerns
  • Responding promptly to all reports of child safety violations
  • Cooperating with law enforcement and relevant authorities
  • Maintaining a safe environment for all users, especially minors

2. Age Restrictions and User Safety

2.1 Minimum Age Requirement

Bible Community is not intended for children under the age of 13. Users must be at least 13 years old to create an account and use our services.

2.2 Age Verification

During account creation, users must confirm they are at least 13 years old. We rely on user-provided information for age verification.

2.3 Parental Guidance

We encourage parents and guardians to monitor their children's use of our app and to discuss online safety practices with them.

3. Prohibited Content and Behavior

The following content and behaviors are strictly prohibited on Bible Community:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) - Any content depicting or promoting child sexual abuse is strictly prohibited
  • Sexual exploitation of minors - Any attempt to exploit, groom, or harm minors is prohibited
  • Inappropriate contact with minors - Adults attempting inappropriate contact with minors is prohibited
  • Harmful content targeting children - Any content designed to harm, exploit, or endanger children
  • Age misrepresentation - Adults misrepresenting their age to contact minors

4. Content Moderation and Safety Measures

4.1 Automated Monitoring

We use automated systems and manual review processes to detect and remove prohibited content, including:

  • Automated content scanning for prohibited material
  • User behavior monitoring for suspicious activity
  • Regular content audits and reviews

4.2 Human Review

All reported content is reviewed by our moderation team. Reports of child safety concerns are prioritized and reviewed immediately.

4.3 Content Removal

Any content that violates our child safety standards is immediately removed, and the user account is permanently banned and reported to authorities.

5. Reporting Child Safety Concerns

5.1 How to Report

If you encounter content or behavior that violates our child safety standards, please report it immediately through one of the following methods:

  • In-App Reporting: Use the reporting feature within the app (when available) to report inappropriate content or users
  • Email Reporting: Send a detailed report to bophelompopo22@gmail.com with the subject "Child Safety Report"
  • Emergency Situations: For immediate threats to a child's safety, contact local law enforcement immediately

5.2 What to Include in Reports

When reporting, please include:

  • Description of the content or behavior
  • Username or account information of the violator
  • Screenshots or evidence (if available and safe to obtain)
  • Date and time of the incident
  • Any other relevant information

5.3 Report Processing

All child safety reports are:

  • Reviewed immediately (within 24 hours)
  • Investigated thoroughly
  • Referred to law enforcement when appropriate
  • Followed up with appropriate action

Important: If you believe a child is in immediate danger, contact your local law enforcement agency immediately. Do not wait to report through our app.

6. Cooperation with Authorities

6.1 Law Enforcement

Bible Community fully cooperates with law enforcement agencies and relevant authorities in investigations related to child safety. We:

  • Preserve evidence related to child safety violations
  • Provide information to law enforcement when legally required
  • Report suspected CSAM to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant authorities
  • Comply with all legal requirements and court orders

6.2 Regional and National Reporting

We comply with all applicable laws regarding reporting child sexual abuse material to:

  • National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) - United States
  • Relevant national authorities in other jurisdictions
  • International law enforcement agencies when appropriate

7. User Education and Prevention

7.1 Safety Guidelines

We provide safety guidelines to help users, especially minors, stay safe on our platform:

  • Never share personal information (address, phone number, school name) with strangers
  • Be cautious when interacting with users you don't know
  • Report suspicious behavior immediately
  • Tell a trusted adult if you feel uncomfortable or threatened

7.2 Community Guidelines

All users must follow our Community Guidelines, which prohibit:

  • Harassment, bullying, or threatening behavior
  • Sharing inappropriate or harmful content
  • Attempting to contact minors inappropriately
  • Any behavior that endangers children

8. Account Actions and Enforcement

8.1 Violation Consequences

Users who violate our child safety standards face immediate and severe consequences:

  • Immediate account suspension - Accounts are suspended immediately upon detection of child safety violations
  • Permanent ban - Violators are permanently banned from the platform
  • Law enforcement reporting - All child safety violations are reported to appropriate authorities
  • Content removal - All violating content is immediately removed

8.2 Zero Tolerance Policy

Bible Community has a zero-tolerance policy for child sexual abuse material and exploitation. There are no warnings or second chances for these violations.

10. Compliance and Legal Requirements

10.1 Legal Compliance

Bible Community complies with all relevant child safety laws, including:

  • United States federal laws regarding child sexual abuse material
  • International laws and conventions on child protection
  • Regional and national reporting requirements
  • Platform-specific safety requirements (Google Play, App Store)

10.2 Regular Review

We regularly review and update our child safety standards to ensure they remain effective and compliant with evolving laws and best practices.
