Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

At Bible Community, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Name or display name
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Password (encrypted and securely stored)

1.2 Content You Create

We collect content you create and share on our platform:

  • Posts and comments in communities
  • Prayer requests and prayers
  • Testimonies and stories
  • Bible study notes
  • Media files (images, videos) you upload

1.3 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your device and usage:

  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • App usage data (features used, time spent)
  • IP address and general location data
  • Crash reports and error logs

1.4 Prayer Reminders

If you set up prayer reminders, we collect:

  • Reminder times and schedules
  • Email address (for email notifications)
  • Timezone information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Create and manage your account
  • Send you prayer reminders and notifications
  • Facilitate community interactions and content sharing
  • Personalize your experience and content recommendations
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

3.1 Firebase (Google)

Our app uses Firebase for:

  • User authentication and account management
  • Cloud database (Firestore) for storing your data
  • Cloud storage for media files
  • Push notifications

Firebase's privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

3.2 Google Books API

We use Google Books API to provide book information and metadata in our bookstore feature. This service may collect usage data according to Google's privacy policy.

Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.3 Affiliate Links

Our bookstore may contain affiliate links. When you click on these links, third-party services (such as Amazon) may collect information according to their own privacy policies. We do not control these third-party services.

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication using Firebase Auth
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

5.1 Access and Portability

You can access, update, or delete your personal information through the app settings or by contacting us.

5.2 Deletion

You can delete your account at any time. When you delete your account, we will delete your personal information, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

5.3 Opt-Out

You can opt-out of:

  • Email notifications (in app settings)
  • Push notifications (in device settings)
  • Prayer reminders (in Prayer Time settings)

5.4 Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data by contacting us at the email address provided below.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: support@biblecommunity.app

Or through the app's support feature in Settings

By using Bible Community, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
