## What is a Feature Graphic?

**Feature Graphic** is a **banner image** that appears at the **top of your app's listing page** on Google Play Store. It's the first thing users see when they visit your app page.

### Key Details:
- **Size:** 1024 x 500 pixels (required)
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG
- **Purpose:** Attract users and showcase your app's value
- **Location:** Top of your Google Play Store listing

---

## ğŸ¯ What to Focus On When Designing

### 1. **App Name & Tagline** (Most Important!)
- **App Name:** "Bible Community" (large, bold, readable)
- **Tagline:** "Connect, Pray, and Grow Together" (smaller, below name)
- **Why:** Users need to know what app they're looking at immediately

### 2. **Visual Appeal**
- **Colors:** Use your app's brand colors (blues, whites, maybe gold/yellow accents)
- **Background:** Clean, not too busy (gradient or solid color works well)
- **Typography:** Bold, readable fonts (avoid fancy script fonts)

### 3. **Key Benefits** (Optional but Recommended)
- Show 2-3 key features:
  - "Daily Prayers"
  - "Bible Study"
  - "Community Support"
- Use icons or small text

### 4. **App Icon** (Optional)
- Include a small version of your app icon
- Helps with brand recognition

## ğŸ’¡ Quick Design Tips

### 1. **Keep it Simple**
- Less is more
- Don't overcrowd with text or images
- White space is your friend

### 2. **Make Text Readable**
- Use bold, clear fonts
- Ensure good contrast (dark text on light background)
- Test: Can you read it from 3 feet away?

### 3. **Use Your Brand Colors**
- Match your app icon colors
- Creates consistency
- Builds brand recognition

### 4. **Show Value**
- What does your app do?
- Why should users download it?
- Keep it concise

### 5. **Mobile-First**
- Remember: Users see this on their phones
- Text should be large enough to read on small screens
- Icons should be clear and recognizable
